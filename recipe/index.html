The Perfect Salad

Description

This Salad is my favorite because of the combination of the ingredients in it.

Ingredients

  • Romain Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cranberry
  • Beets
  • Corns
  • Avocado
  • Chicken

Directions

  1. Shredded the romain lettuce into small pieces.
  2. Diced the tomato into small square size.
  3. Dried up a small portion of cranberries in a toaster.
  4. Chopped the beets into small size.
  5. A small portion of corn grains(Maize).
  6. Peeled and sliced an avocado.
  7. Grilled a chicken breast to perfection.
  8. Chopped the grilled chicken into small pieces.
  9. Add a little salad dressing and Mixed everything in a bowl.
